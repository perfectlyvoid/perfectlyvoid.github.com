Contour configurator let's you change the settings of your contour camera and synchronizes it's internal clock, without the necessity to plug it into a computer.

It's targeted for ROAM2 cameras. Usage with other models is possible, though may be limited.

FAQ

After pressing the "push" button and unplugging the device the configuration isn't saved. Why is that?

File writes are not persisted to storage immediately on android. We are working on a solution to ensure that the configuration is written properly, but until we find that, please wait 5-10 seconds before disconnecting the camera from your device.
In case of using just the sd card - make sure you unmount it before removing from your device.

I get a message saying "The detected configuration version is unrecognized. This may cause configuration corruption." Should I be worried?

This warning is displayed when the camera configuration version is unknown to the application. The configuration update tries to be as noninvasive as possible, but in some situations, writing configuration in an unrecognized version may lead to it's corruption.
To restore a valid configuration, connect the camera to your computer and use the software provided by contour or, backup the data on your cameras' sd card and format it.
